Liquid Glass Accordion

A self-contained collapsible panel with glass aesthetics. Each angular-liquid-glass-accordion manages its own open/close state independently — no container component required.

Liquid Glass is a design language by Apple that combines frosted glass, SVG turbulence distortion, and specular highlights to simulate a refractive glass surface.

Each component appends a hidden SVG filter to the document body and applies it via filter: url(#id) combined with backdrop-filter to distort and blur the content behind the glass surface.

Full effect requires Chrome. Other browsers will display a simplified frosted glass version without the SVG distortion filter.
